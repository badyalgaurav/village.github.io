    OUR STORY AT VILLAGE

    We first opened our doors 40 years ago, in Sowerby Bridge. Over the last 4 decades, our ancestors have invested their sweat and blood in making this restaurant a Majestic Indian Restaurant. We have served served customers with great food and customer services and we can not thank them enough for being with us for the last 40 years.

    This is our website, and we encourage all of you who have dined at our establishment to share our site across your social media, as well as spreading the message with your friends and family.
